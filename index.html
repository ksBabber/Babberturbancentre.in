Turban Styles

Patiala Shahi Dastar

Patiala Shahi Pagg is a turban style based upon the turban styles of old kings.This is also known as Tehdi pagg. It is the most common among all the turbans and usually worn in the north part of India, especially in Punjab.

Double Larhi Dastar

This is a creative turban style in which single larh is divided into two larh. People love to tie this style occasionally. This style is one of the famous styles among royal people.We can tie this style in many forms including Patiala Shahi, Morni, Semi Patiala Shahi.

Wattan wali Dastar

This is a very common Sikh Turban style and this turban is also based upon old style. It is very easy to tie up. This style is also known as free style turban. It is divided in the middle due to the amount of wraps(watt) it rounds at the top.

3D Dastar

This is a new style of turban and is widely famous among the new generation.This style is tie up by giving a new 3d look to our normal Patiala Shahi, Semi patiala shahi or Morni Dastar. Sikh People mostly wore this style during different occations, competitions .

Triple Larh Dastar

This Dastar style is also a creative style as double larhi dastar. In this Style we usually divide one larh into three instead of two. This style is widely famous in the youngsters who love to wear different styles of turbans.
